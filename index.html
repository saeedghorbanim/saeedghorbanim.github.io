Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track an expense?

You can track expenses in three easy ways: use voice input to speak your expense, scan a receipt with your camera, or manually enter the details.

How do I manage my subscription?

To view, modify, or cancel your SaviCash Premium subscription:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone or iPad
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Select SaviCash Premium
  5. Choose your desired option (modify or cancel)

You can also manage your subscription directly in the SaviCash app by going to Settings > Subscription.

Is my financial data secure?

Yes, absolutely. We take your privacy seriously. All your data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your personal financial information with third parties. For more details, please review our Privacy Policy.

What's included in SaviCash Premium?

SaviCash Premium includes:

  • Unlimited expense tracking
  • Advanced budgeting insights
  • Unlimited receipt scanning
  • Custom categories and tags
  • Export and reporting features
  • Priority customer support
