<h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

<h3>How do I track an expense?</h3>
<p>You can track expenses in three easy ways: use voice input to speak your expense, scan a receipt with your camera, or manually enter the details.</p>

<h3>How do I manage my subscription?</h3>
<p>To view, modify, or cancel your SaviCash Premium subscription:</p>
<ol>
    <li>Open the <strong>Settings</strong> app on your iPhone or iPad</li>
    <li>Tap your name at the top</li>
    <li>Tap <strong>Subscriptions</strong></li>
    <li>Select <strong>SaviCash Premium</strong></li>
    <li>Choose your desired option (modify or cancel)</li>
</ol>
<p>You can also manage your subscription directly in the SaviCash app by going to Settings > Subscription.</p>

<h3>Is my financial data secure?</h3>
<p>Yes, absolutely. We take your privacy seriously. All your data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your personal financial information with third parties. For more details, please review our <a href="privacy-policy.html">Privacy Policy</a>.</p>
